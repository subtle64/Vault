About Us

See what makes ILKEA different from other brands.


Our History


Established since 2010, ILKEA puts a strong emphasis on furnitures that are both stylish but also functional in the modern age.

ILKEA was a project conceived by the mind of Jason Adriel, who in 2007, set out to build one of the greatest furniture brands in Indonesia. ILKEA started off as a small company, but soon grew into the great company you see today, with branches in Jakarta, Surabaya, and Bali.


High Quality Woods


No furniture is complete without proper materials. ILKEA furnitures use high quality mahogany, Engelmann spruce, rosewood, or ebony wood for its wooden craftmanship. Sourced from sustainable sources, we commit to provide the best wood quality whilst also protecting the environment.

Our woods are sourced from various rainforest certified sources. Each block of wood is inspected thorougly for any material issues or cracks by our strict quality control. Only master-grade wood cuts are allowed in ILKEA. Trust us on giving you the best materials for your furnitures.


Military Grade Steel


Who says only the military uses steel? Our furnitures are reinforced with high quality steel to ensure that our furnitures are not only elegant to look at, but also last for decades of use. Any critical furniture that we have have steel reinforcements to ensure the safety and longetivity of it.

Our steel is certified MIL-46177 steel plates sourced from an ISO certified company. We are committed for the longetivity, and more importantly, the safety of our materials in our furnitures.
