Elegant craftsmanship.


Our humble beginnings started when Jason Adriel, an excellent carpenter and woodworker envisioned a furniture brand distinct from the others. He envisioned ILKEA full of elegantly crafted furnitures perfect for modern homes, yet he wanted to include the streak of unique Indonesian heritage into it.

With that notion, ILKEA was born. ILKEA has worked nonstop for almost 14 years to provide the highest quality furniture for any homes. We plan to keep it that way for decades to come.
